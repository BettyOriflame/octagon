The Spot

Warri's waterfront, reimagined as a night out.

Octagon sits on the creek in Warri GRA, built as one compound with several rooms to move through in a single evening — quiet sit-out by the water, a cooled lounge, an indoor games room, and a club floor that wakes up after dark. Guests come back for the scenery as much as the menu.

🌊

Creek-Side Sit-Out

Open-air seating with a direct sea/creek view — the most-mentioned reason guests say they keep returning.

🍽️

Eatery & Bakery

A full kitchen known for its shawarma and fish dishes, plus an on-site bakery guests describe as smelling as good as it tastes.

🥂

VIP Lounge

An air-conditioned lounge and VIP section for guests who want the night a little more private, a little more cooled-down.

🎮

Indoor Games Room

Pool table and indoor games for the hours between sundown and the club floor filling up.

🎶

Octagon Nightclub

The compound's late-night room — sound, lights and a dance floor once the eatery crowd thins out.

🏛️

Event Centre

A multipurpose hall on-site for private parties, hangouts and functions, wheelchair-accessible from the entrance.
